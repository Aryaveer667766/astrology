Frequently Asked Questions

Are my details kept private?
Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All personal information and consultation details shared with astrologers are kept 100% confidential.
How does the payment system work?
We use a direct UPI system to avoid hidden gateway charges. You make the payment to our secure UPI ID, submit the 12-digit UTR, and our admin credits the balance to your account instantly.
What if my chat gets disconnected?
The session is actively timed. If ended early, you only pay for the exact minutes spent connected.
Can I rate the astrologer after a session?
Yes! After every session, you'll be prompted to rate and review the astrologer. Your feedback helps others make informed choices.
